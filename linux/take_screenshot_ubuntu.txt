Take a Screenshot of the Whole Screen
Simply press the Print Screen button on your keyboard to capture a clip of the entire screen. The screenshot will be auto-saved in the Pictures directory.

Capture a Specific Area in Ubuntu
You might find yourself in situations when you only have to capture a specific section of the entire screen---may be a dialog box, something specific on your browser, etc.

In such cases, press Shift and Print Screen together to capture the screenshot.
